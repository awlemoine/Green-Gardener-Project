Marketing Analysis

Many people don’t have the time or space to grow their own fruits and vegetables and suffer from diabetes and obesity due to a lack of fresh and organic produce in their diets. Also, large amounts of landfill waste pollute the atmosphere, contributing to the ever-growing threat of global warming.

The Green Gardener waters plants, plants seeds, harvests, and takes care of any other gardening needs. It composts waste into garden soil and deposits it into the garden.
